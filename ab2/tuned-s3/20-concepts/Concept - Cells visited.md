# Cells visited

Cells Visited

## In training (Doom)

In training an agent to play Doom, cells visited refers to the number of unique locations or areas within the game environment that have been traversed by the agent during a single episode. This metric is indicative of the exploration efficiency and scope of the agent's understanding of the game world.
