# Distance episode

The concept of 'Distance Episode' refers to a metric used during training an agent to play Doom, representing the total distance traversed by the agent within each episode. This metric provides insight into the agent's exploration and movement capabilities in the game environment.

## In training (Doom)

During training, as the agent learns to navigate through various levels of Doom, it will traverse different distances depending on its strategies and actions. The total distance traveled per episode can be used to evaluate how far the agent has moved within a single level or map, indicating improvements in movement efficiency, pathfinding, or evasion skills.
