# Action Distribution Stability

Action Distribution Stability

## In training (Doom)

In the training of an agent to play Doom, the concept of Action Distribution Stability manifests as a consistent pattern in the actions chosen by the agent. The most frequently used actions are TURN_LEFT and TURN_RIGHT, constituting approximately 43% and 29%, respectively.
