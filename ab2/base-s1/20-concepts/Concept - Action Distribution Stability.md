# Action Distribution Stability

Action Distribution Stability

## In training (Doom)

In training an agent to play Doom, the action distribution stability concept refers to the consistency in the frequency of actions taken by the agent over multiple checkpoints. Specifically, the analysis indicates that TURN_LEFT (46%) and ATTACK (32%) remain relatively stable, with a slight variation in TURN_RIGHT (21%). This suggests that these actions continue to be among the most frequently used by the agent.
