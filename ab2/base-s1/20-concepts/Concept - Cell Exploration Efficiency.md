# Cell Exploration Efficiency

Cell Exploration Efficiency refers to the proportion of cells or areas in a game environment, such as Doom, that an agent visits during its training. It is measured against the total number of cells available for exploration.

## In training (Doom)

In the context of training agents to play Doom, Cell Exploration Efficiency manifests when the agent successfully navigates and interacts with various rooms, corridors, and other areas within the game environment. A low efficiency rate indicates that the agent is not exploring a significant portion of the available space, potentially missing out on critical information or objectives.
